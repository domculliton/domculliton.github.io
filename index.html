My Thermomix Story

Thermomix Consultant based in Prosper, Texas — serving Dallas, DFW, and customers across the USA.

My story with Thermomix started long before I became a consultant.

I first discovered Thermomix in the 1990s, when I was a little girl visiting my aunt in Poland. She hosted a demo at her home and bought her very first Thermomix that day. And here's the amazing part — that machine is still working today. She's simply upgraded to a newer model.

For many years, I wanted my own Thermomix, but like many families, we kept saying: "It's amazing… but it's expensive."

Everything changed after the birth of my child. My husband surprised me with a Thermomix as a push present. It was a TM5 — and yes, one month later the TM6 was released!

Soon after, we hosted a barbecue at our house. While the guys were grilling outside, I stayed in the kitchen preparing salads, dips, and cocktails — all in my Thermomix. In about 20 minutes, everything was ready.

"What is that machine? How did you make all of this so fast?"

None of my friends had heard of Thermomix — and several wanted one. That moment changed everything.

I signed up as a consultant, earned my Thermomix right away, and have been part of this journey ever since — including 3 years as a consultant in Ireland. Today I continue my business here in the United States, helping families nationwide from my home in Prosper, Texas.

I'm a busy mom of three — one boy and two girls — so I understand real life and real family needs: quick weeknight meals, healthy options for kids, lunchbox ideas, budget-friendly cooking, and saving time and reducing daily stress. Thermomix fits every bit of that.

Thermomix FAQs

How long does a demo last?
Most demos run about 60–90 minutes. We cook a couple of dishes together, I walk you through the features, and you get to ask as many questions as you like. No rush, no pressure.
Is the demo really free?
Absolutely. There is zero cost and zero obligation. I love showing off Thermomix — whether you buy or not, I hope you leave inspired!
Does Thermomix offer financing?
Yes! Thermomix partners with third-party financing providers Bread and Affirm to offer flexible payment plans, so you can spread the cost comfortably. Options are shown at checkout on the official Thermomix site — ask me during your demo and I'll walk you through it.
Can I become a consultant too?
Yes, and I'd love to have you on my team! Visit the Join My Team page to learn more about the opportunity and how I support every person I bring on board.
Do you do Zoom demos?
Yes! Zoom demos are a great option if you're not local or prefer to watch from home. They're just as interactive — and I'll still be cooking live on camera.
