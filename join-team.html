Consultant FAQs

Do I have to buy a Thermomix to join?
Thermomix consultants typically need a personal unit to demo from — you can purchase one at a reduced consultant price when you sign up. Ask me for the current details.
How much can I earn?
Earnings vary based on the number of demos you host and sales you make. Thermomix offers a transparent commission structure. I'm happy to share what I earn personally when we chat — no fluff, just real numbers.
Is there a minimum sales target?
There's no aggressive quota system. You work your business at your own pace. I'll always give you honest guidance about what's realistic.
Can I do it alongside my full-time job?
Absolutely. Many consultants run their Thermomix business in evenings and weekends. It's fully flexible — you set your own schedule.
What does Thermomix provide vs. what do you provide?
Thermomix provides the official training, Cookidoo access, marketing materials, and commission structure. I provide personal mentorship, real-world demo tips, community, and ongoing day-to-day support.
